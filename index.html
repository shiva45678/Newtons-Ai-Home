How They Work

Simple, powerful workflows for doctors and hospitals.

1 OPD Assistant

Input chief complaints. AI suggests questions, generates differentials, and summarizes history for your review.

2 Treatment Planner

Get standard treatment guidelines, dosage calculations, and drug interaction alerts. You manually prescribe.

3 ICU Assistant

Input ABG/Vitals. AI predicts deterioration risk and suggests stabilization protocols.

1 Voice Intake

Patient talks to AI in local language. Doctor approves. HMS auto-orders meds and tests.

2 Lab Loop

HMS pulls data from Analyzers (e.g., CBC). AI reads reports, auto-edits treatment, and updates orders.

3 Inventory & Billing

Stock deducted in real-time. A consolidated bill is generated automatically at discharge.
