How They Work

1. OPD Assistant

Input chief complaints. AI suggests questions, generates differentials, and summarizes history for your review.

2. Treatment Planner

Get standard treatment guidelines, dosage calculations, and drug interaction alerts. You manually prescribe.

3. ICU Assistant

Manually input ABG/Vitals. AI predicts deterioration risk and suggests stabilization protocols.

1. Voice Intake & Auto-Ordering

Patient talks to AI in local language. Doctor approves plan. HMS auto-orders meds from pharmacy and tests from internal lab.

2. Lab Loop & Machine Integration

HMS pulls data directly from Analyzers (e.g., CBC). AI reads the report, auto-edits the treatment plan (e.g., changes antibiotics), and updates orders.

3. Inventory & Billing

Stock is deducted from Pharmacy/Lab inventory in real-time as orders are executed. A single consolidated bill is generated automatically.
